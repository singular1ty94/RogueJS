RogueJS

A Javascript Roguelike by singular1ty94.

PLAY

Skills

Perception

Rank 1 Increase torch strength by 2 cells.
Rank 2 Sense items and stairs in the level.
Rank 3 Sense enemies through the walls.

Weapon Art

Rank 1 Weapons deal more damage.
Rank 2 You take less damage from enemies.
Rank 3 The first strike against an enemy deals double damage.

Arcana

Rank 1 Gain more XP per enemy kill.
Rank 2 Gain some HP back from killing an enemy.
Rank 3 Fully recover HP from changing levels.
